Privacy Policy

At ELIE SANON LLC, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, email, phone number, and business details when you register, fill out a form, or interact with our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to deliver personalized consulting services, improve user experience, process payments, and communicate important updates. We do not sell or share your data without consent.

3. Data Security

Your data security is our top priority. We implement robust technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access or disclosure.

4. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to enhance functionality and analyze web traffic. You may choose to disable cookies via your browser settings, though this may affect site usability.

5. Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party tools (e.g., payment gateways) that comply with privacy regulations. These providers only access data necessary to perform services on our behalf.

6. Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your data by contacting us. As a U.S.-based firm, we adhere to applicable federal and state privacy laws including CCPA and GDPR for international users.

7. Updates to this Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy at any time. We encourage users to review this page regularly to stay informed.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related concerns, please contact us at contact@eliesanon.com or visit our office at 6130 Elton Avenue, Las Vegas, NV 89107, USA.
